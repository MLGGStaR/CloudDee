About CloudDee

CloudDee is an editorial channel publishing documentary-style breakdowns of newly released US public records — NTSB aviation and marine accident reports, federal court opinions, Department of Justice press releases, and SEC enforcement actions. Every video is sourced to the original public-record document, which is cited in the description.

Narration is synthesized by AI from editorial scripts written from the cited primary sources. Visuals combine licensed stock imagery with AI-generated illustrations. The channel discloses this on every upload.

Editorial Standards

  • Every fact is drawn from the cited public-record document.
  • Allegations against uncharged or unconvicted parties are characterized as allegations, consistent with the underlying source.
  • Sealed cases, identified minors, and matters of sexual violence against identifiable victims are filtered out before publication.
  • Corrections are accepted via the comments on each video and addressed publicly.
