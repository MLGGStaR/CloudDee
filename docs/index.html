What CloudDee Is

CloudDee is an editorial channel that turns dense, jargon-heavy public-record documents into clear, watchable case files for general audiences. We focus on records that were freshly released in the last 72 hours by federal agencies and federal courts of record.

Each video is anchored to a specific, citable public document — an NTSB accident report, a federal indictment, an SEC litigation release, a DOJ press release. The document's URL is printed in every video description. Nothing in our videos is fabricated: every fact traces back to the cited source.

Editorial Standards

  • Source-anchored. Every fact traces to the cited public-record document. No off-source claims, no speculation presented as fact.
  • Presumption of innocence. Allegations against uncharged or unconvicted parties are characterized as allegations ("alleged," "according to the indictment"), matching how the underlying source frames them.
  • Filtered exclusions. Sealed cases, proceedings with identified minors, and matters involving sexual violence against identifiable victims are filtered out before scripting.
  • AI-content disclosure. Narration is synthesized via text-to-speech from editorial scripts; some imagery is AI-generated. Disclosed in every video description and in each upload's platform metadata.
  • Public corrections. Errors are corrected via video-description edits and pinned correction comments. We do not silently rewrite history.

FAQ

How often do you publish?

Approximately one long-form documentary (5–10 minutes) and three Shorts (under 60 seconds each) per day, seven days a week.

Is this human-written or AI-generated?

The editorial pipeline is automated. Scripts are written from the cited primary-source documents by an AI language model under a documented editorial system prompt. Narration is then synthesized via text-to-speech. Some imagery is AI-generated; the rest is licensed stock photography. This is disclosed on every video.

I'm named in a video. How do I request a correction or removal?

Email thememesofdoom@gmail.com with the video URL and the basis for the request. We respond to verified corrections within 7 business days. We do not gatekeep names that appear in unsealed public records, but we will edit framing, descriptions, or remove videos where the request is reasonable.

Are you affiliated with any agency you cover?

No. CloudDee is independent of NTSB, DOJ, SEC, the federal judiciary, and any other agency whose records we cover. No relationship, no funding, no editorial input from any of them.

The full archive lives on our YouTube channel. See the Episodes page for the most recent.
