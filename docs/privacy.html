Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how CloudDee handles information in connection with the CloudDee website (this site) and the editorial channel published under the CloudDee brand on YouTube.

1. Information CloudDee collects

CloudDee does not directly collect any personal information from visitors to this website. We do not run analytics, advertising, or tracking scripts on these pages. We do not set cookies.

When you watch a CloudDee video on YouTube, the platform (YouTube, not CloudDee) collects information from you according to its own privacy policy. Please refer to the YouTube / Google Privacy Policy.

2. Public records used as source content

CloudDee video content is based on documents publicly released by US Government agencies and courts. These public records often contain personally identifying information (names, addresses, license numbers, case numbers) that was published by the original source agency, not by CloudDee. CloudDee uses this information only as it appears in the public-record source.

Specifically, CloudDee filters out and does not feature: sealed cases, proceedings involving identified minors, and matters of sexual violence against identifiable victims. Allegations against uncharged or unconvicted parties are characterized as allegations.

3. Removal requests for individuals named in public records

If you are named in a CloudDee video because you are named in the underlying public record, and you believe its inclusion is unfair or inaccurate, please contact us at thememesofdoom@gmail.com. We evaluate such requests in good faith and will edit or remove content where the request is reasonable.

4. Information CloudDee uses to operate the channel

To publish videos to YouTube, CloudDee uses authentication tokens issued by those platforms to the channel owner. These tokens are stored as encrypted GitHub Actions secrets and are used solely to upload videos and post related metadata (titles, descriptions, captions) to the channel owner's own accounts. No third-party user data is involved.

5. AI-generated content

CloudDee narration audio is generated by AI text-to-speech from editorial scripts. Some video imagery is AI-generated. This is disclosed in every video description and in each upload's platform metadata. No real person's voice is impersonated, and no real person's likeness is generated.

6. Changes to this Privacy Policy

CloudDee may revise this Policy at any time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.

7. Contact

For privacy questions or data-handling concerns: thememesofdoom@gmail.com.
